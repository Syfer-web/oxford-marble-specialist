## 🔴 CRITICAL — Fix before launch

### 1. "Refit" brand leak in testimonials section
**Line ~61336** — Section header reads:
> "Hear from our happy clients about their experience working with **Refit** and the quality of our craftsmanship."

"Refit" is the original template's brand name. This is a major trust-killer — every visitor sees it. **Replace with "Oxford Marble Specialist" or "OMS"**.

### 2. Contact form does nothing — leads vanish
The big "Get a quote" modal goes through 5 beautiful steps (stonework type → material → dimensions → extras → contact), validates the email locally, and shows a green "Message sent!" checkmark. **There is no `fetch()`, no `mailto:`, no Formspree, no backend.** Every lead the site captures is lost.

**Fix:** Wire to Formspree (free, 50/month) or Basin ($9/mo, 100/mo). Need Saad's choice of email destination first.

### 3. "100% client satisfaction" + "Every client would recommend us" fabricated
**Line ~similar area** — A counter shows "0%" with target "100%" under "Client satisfaction" → "Every client would recommend us without hesitation." This is unprovable until OMS has 50+ real reviews. Risk: ASA / Trading Standards complaint territory for a UK service business.

**Fix:** Replace with something provable ("Free quotes" / "Yrs experience: TBC" / "Slabs sourced: 100+") or remove the stat block entirely.

### 4. The "100% client satisfaction" stat counter starts at 0%
The counter is a CSS animation that animates from 0 to 100%. A new visitor sees it counting up to "100%" — and the value of "100%" itself is unprovable. Same fix as #3.

### 5. No `<meta name="description">`
Title is set: "Oxford Marble Specialist | Premium Stone Worktops Oxfordshire & London". But the description tag is missing. Google search results will auto-generate one (usually badly). Same for OG/Twitter card descriptions — they fall back to title only.

### 6. Phone number is a placeholder
`+44789 123456` is in the wizard placeholder. Real phone is `07716534984` (shown elsewhere). The wizard placeholder text becomes a copy-paste target for users. Use the real number in placeholders.

### 7. Email placeholder is fake
`johnsmith@gmail.com` — placeholder text. Same issue as phone. Use a real address (TBC by Saad).

## 🟠 HIGH — Should fix

### 8. Social icons don't link
Footer has Instagram/Facebook/Twitter icons (rounded circles with SVG glyphs), but no `href` — they're just `<div class="soc">` with `cursor:pointer`. Clicks do nothing. Either add real social URLs or remove the icons entirely (don't fake them).

### 9. No aria-labels on social icons
Even when fixed, screen readers will see "button" with no name. Add `aria-label="Follow OMS on Instagram"`.

### 10. Testimonials are plausibly fabricated
6 testimonials with first-name + area (Chelsea, Richmond, etc.) but no last names, no project photos that match the "Calacatta marble worktop" descriptions, and the text was clearly written by the same person. Risk: if a real client sees a fake testimonial attributed to their area, they will (a) call you out publicly and (b) report to ASA.

**Fix options:**
- (a) Mark them as samples with a banner above the carousel: "Sample testimonials — we'll feature real client reviews here once projects complete"
- (b) Wait for 3-5 real testimonials before launching this section
- (c) Replace with case-study cards linked to the gallery (real project, no fake person)

### 11. The gallery "Our Work" section is generic stock
8 Framer CDN stock images. The hero promises "Oxford's premier stone masonry specialists" but the gallery shows stock kitchens, not OMS's actual work. Visitors will notice. Either (a) replace with real OMS project photos, (b) add a disclaimer ("Portfolio photos coming soon — placeholder images"), or (c) reduce the section to a "Book a showroom visit" CTA.

### 12. No thank-you / fallback after form submit
Even after #2 is fixed, there's no error state if the form backend is down. Add a "We couldn't send your message — please call us" fallback with a click-to-call link.

## 🟡 MEDIUM — Polish

### 13. Cookie banner: no reject-all option
The cookie banner has Accept/Decline buttons (good), but Decline likely just hides the banner. If you set any analytics, the decline button should clear cookies. Test this.

### 14. FAQ open-state by default
First FAQ item has `class="faq-item open"` — opens on load. Most sites keep all closed and let user click. Mildly annoying.

### 15. 404 page is a separate template
`404.html` exists with its own inline styles (404 lines, 69 KB) but the rest of the site is in `index.html` (1,632 lines, 226 KB). Three separate HTML files (privacy, terms, 404) each with their own full `<style>` block = harder to maintain, no shared navigation. Consider extracting to a single shared CSS file.

### 16. No structured data / schema.org
No `LocalBusiness` or `Organization` JSON-LD. Google won't show your business hours, location, or service area in search results. Easy win — 30 lines of JSON-LD.

### 17. No sitemap reference in robots.txt
`sitemap.xml` exists, but `robots.txt` is empty (81 bytes). Add `Sitemap: https://syfer-client-test.dplooy.com/sitemap.xml`.

### 18. Privacy Policy + Terms are generic
Look like template fills ("Lorem ipsum of legal pages"). They probably don't reference actual data practices (what happens to form submissions? cookie usage?). Review needed.

### 19. "Oxfordshire & London" — confirm service area
The page claims to serve both Oxfordshire AND London. With one workshop and presumably 1-2 fitters, that's a 90+ mile radius. Confirm with Saad if this is realistic, or narrow to one region.

### 22. WhatsApp link is the only external
`https://wa.me/447716534984` works (assumes 44 + 7716534984 = 447716534984). Add `target="_blank"` and `rel="noopener"`.

### 23. Year in footer says 2025
`© 2025 Oxford Marble Specialist` — should be auto-updating to 2026. Add `<span id="year">` + JS.

### 24. "Book a visit" CTA in service area
Once you have a real address / showroom, add a CTA. For now, "Visit our Oxford workshop" is a claim.

### 25. No favicon for Safari pinned tabs
`apple-touch-icon.png` exists. Good. But no `safari-pinned-tab.svg` and corresponding `<link rel="mask-icon" color="#b8964e">`. Optional.
