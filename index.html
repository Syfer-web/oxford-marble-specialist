Services

What we do

Every surface tells a story — let Oxford Marble Specialist craft yours in stone

Kitchen Worktops

Our signature service. We template, fabricate and install bespoke kitchen worktops in Marble, Granite, Quartz and Quartzite. Every slab is hand-selected for grain and colour consistency, precision-cut with water-jet and CNC technology, then hand-finished by our stonemasons. We handle island tops, breakfast bars, upstands and splashbacks as a complete package.

Bathroom Stone

Transform your bathroom into a sanctuary with natural stone. We supply and install vanity tops, bath surrounds, shower walls, niches and window sills in Marble, Travertine and Limestone. Our honed and polished finishes are sealed to resist moisture, keeping surfaces pristine for decades.

Stone Flooring

Limestone, Slate, Sandstone and Marble flooring installed to the highest standard. We prepare sub-floors, lay stone with precision joints, grout and seal to a professional finish. Whether large-format slabs for a contemporary look or traditional flagstones, we work to your exact specification.

Feature Walls & Cladding

Stone cladding and feature walls create dramatic focal points in living rooms, hallways and behind fireplaces. We work with bookmatched marble panels, split-face slate, stacked ledgestone and full-slab polished surfaces. Interior and exterior cladding expertly fixed and sealed.

Custom & Bespoke Stonework

Have something unique in mind? Our workshop can fabricate almost anything from stone — fire surrounds, hearths, garden water features, bespoke furniture tops, reception desks and signage. We collaborate closely with architects and interior designers to bring one-of-a-kind stone pieces to life.

External Stonework

Natural stone elevates outdoor spaces beautifully. We install patio paving, garden steps, entrance paths, external window sills and copings in frost-resistant Granite, Sandstone and Limestone. All external stone is professionally laid on a mortar bed and sealed against the elements.

FAQs

Answering your questions

Got more questions? Send us your enquiry below

Get in touch
Which stone materials do you work with?
We work with the full range of natural and engineered stones — Marble, Granite, Quartz, Quartzite, Limestone, Travertine, Slate and Sandstone. We source from trusted quarries across Italy, Spain, Brazil and Portugal, and can help you select the right material for your project and budget.
How does the worktop installation process work?
We follow a four-step process: (1) Free consultation and material selection, (2) Precise templating of your space once cabinets are fitted, (3) Fabrication in our workshop — cutting, shaping and polishing to your specification, (4) Professional installation and sealing. Most kitchen worktops take 2–4 weeks from templating to installation.
Do you offer free quotes and consultations?
Yes — all consultations and quotes are completely free with no obligation. We'll visit your home, discuss your ideas, show you samples of relevant stones, and provide a detailed written quote broken down by material and labour. We can also visit our stone suppliers together so you can select your exact slab in person.
How do I care for my stone worktop or surface?
All our stone is professionally sealed at installation. For daily care, simply wipe with a damp cloth and a pH-neutral cleaner — avoid acidic cleaners like vinegar or lemon juice on marble and limestone. We provide full aftercare instructions for each stone type and recommend re-sealing porous stones every 1–3 years. We offer a re-sealing service to all our clients.
Can you match stone across multiple surfaces?
Yes — this is one of our specialisms. For kitchens with islands and perimeter worktops, or bathrooms where the vanity, bath panel and shower walls should flow together, we carefully map the slab before cutting to ensure veining and pattern continue naturally across joins. We can also book-match marble panels for a truly striking symmetrical effect.
Do you work with architects and interior designers?
Absolutely — we work regularly alongside architects, interior designers and main contractors. We can provide DXF files and technical drawings for specification, attend site meetings, and coordinate installation around other trades. Trade accounts with preferential pricing are available for design professionals.
What areas do you cover?
We are based in Abingdon, Oxfordshire and primarily serve Oxfordshire, including Oxford, Witney, Didcot, Wantage and the Cotswolds. We also cover London and the wider Home Counties — Berkshire, Buckinghamshire, Gloucestershire and beyond. Our studio and workshop is at Kingston Business Park, Abingdon — visits by appointment.
